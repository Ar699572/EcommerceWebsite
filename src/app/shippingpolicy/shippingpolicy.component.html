Shipping Policy

To return your product, you can directly do it through our return courier

Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund. Depending on where you live, the time it may take for your exchanged product to reach you, may vary.

Self-Ship

If your Pincode is not in the serviceable area i.e. if our delivery agents are not able to come for pick-up, the customer has to self-ship the product.
